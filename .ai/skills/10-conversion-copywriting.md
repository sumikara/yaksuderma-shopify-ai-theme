# Skill 10 — Conversion Copywriting

## 1. Purpose

This skill is used to create safe, conversion-focused copy for the YaksuDerma Shopify theme.

Use this skill when writing or improving:

- Homepage hero copy
- Trust bar copy
- Category card copy
- Collection descriptions
- Product page microcopy
- Product page accordions
- Support sections
- Wholesale inquiry sections
- Authenticity / certificate sections
- CTA buttons
- Empty states
- Announcement bars
- Footer copy
- Product comparison table labels
- Related product section labels

This skill must always be used together with:

```text
.ai/content-rules.md
```

Conversion copy must improve trust and clarity without creating risky medical, regulatory, shipping, or certificate claims.

## 3. Copywriting Goal

YaksuDerma copy should help users feel:

- The store is trustworthy.
- Products are organized clearly.
- Products are Korean-origin.
- The brand values authenticity.
- Pricing is fair and accessible.
- Support is warm and helpful.
- The store is retail-friendly but professional.
- Business or wholesale inquiries are possible if needed.

The copy should increase confidence through clarity, not pressure.

## 4. Brand Voice

YaksuDerma’s voice should be:

- Warm
- Helpful
- Professional
- Clear
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Human
- Retail-friendly
- International
- Product-discovery focused

The voice should not be:

- Aggressive
- Pushy
- Overly clinical
- Overly luxury
- Overly emotional
- Fear-based
- Claim-heavy
- Marketplace-like
- Cheap-sounding

## 5. Core Message

The core brand message is:

```text
Authentic Korean-origin aesthetics products, organized clearly, offered with fair pricing and helpful customer support.
```

Codex should adapt this message across homepage, collection pages, product pages, and support sections.

## 6. Non-Negotiable Content Safety Rules

Codex must not invent:

- Medical claims
- Treatment outcomes
- Dosage instructions
- Injection instructions
- Regulatory approvals
- Certificate claims
- Country-specific legal claims
- Product safety guarantees
- Before/after claims
- Guaranteed shipping claims
- Manufacturer authorization claims
- Clinical performance claims

Codex must not write copy that implies:

- Guaranteed results
- Instant transformation
- Wrinkle removal promises
- Fat loss promises
- Skin regeneration guarantees
- Permanent effects
- “Use this product this way” instructions
- “Must be used with” medical guidance

When unsure, use neutral product-discovery language or ask for confirmation.

## 22. Related Product Copy

Related products should feel helpful, not instructional.

Preferred labels:

```text
Related products
You may also explore
Often viewed with

```

Avoid:

```text
Required for treatment
Must be used with

Treatment protocol
Procedure bundle
```

Known safe direction:

- Liporase may be presented as a related product for filler products if technically configured.
- Numbing creams may be presented as related accessories.
- Do not frame related products as medical instructions.

## 26. Before / After Copy

Before / after visual sections are sensitive.

Allowed only if:

- User provides verified images
- User confirms compliance
- Copy is neutral
- No guaranteed result is implied
- Section is optional and configurable

Preferred neutral labels:

```text
Visual reference
Product information image
Before / after reference
Customer-provided visual, if verified
```

Avoid:

```text
Guaranteed transformation
This product creates this result
Instant result
Permanent result
Clinically proven before and after
```

Before / after sections should be disabled by default unless approved.

## 27. Copy Length Rules

Keep copy short and mobile-friendly.

Recommended:

```text
Hero headline: 4–8 words
Hero subtext: 1–2 short sentences
Trust bar items: 2–4 words each
Category card subtitle: 1 short sentence
Collection description: 1 short paragraph
Support block: 1–2 short sentences
Product accordion copy: short and expandable
CTA text: 1–4 words
```

Avoid long paragraphs on homepage and product cards.

## 28. SEO Copy Rules

SEO copy should be clear but not claim-heavy.

Use:

- Exact product names
- Clear category names
- Korean-origin product language
- Product discovery language
- Fair pricing language
- Authenticity language where safe

Avoid:

- Keyword stuffing
- Medical claims for SEO
- Fake approval claims
- Repetitive product terms
- Decorative vague category names

Good SEO-safe phrases:

```text
Korean-origin aesthetic products
Botulinum toxins
Dermal fillers
Skin boosters
Exosomes
Fat dissolvers
Clinical accessories
Original product selection
```

## 29. International English Rules

Because YaksuDerma targets Europe first, then Asia and the Middle East, copy should feel internationally understandable.

Use simple, clear English.

Avoid:

- Too much slang
- Country-specific legal claims
- Overly local references
- Complex regulatory language unless verified
- Unclear abbreviations unless they are product names

## 31. Implementation Safety

If Codex is asked to implement copy into the theme:

- Use theme editor settings where possible.
- Do not hardcode copy if it should be editable.
- Avoid placing long copy in Liquid if merchant should manage it.
- Do not add claims.
- Do not change unrelated files.
- Preserve Shopify data and logic.

## 34. Final Reminder

Conversion copy should build trust through clarity, authenticity, fair pricing, and helpful support.

Main principle:

> Write copy that helps customers understand and trust the store — without pressure, exaggeration, or risky claims.
