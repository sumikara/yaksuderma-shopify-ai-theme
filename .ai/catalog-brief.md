# Shopify Store Architecture & SEO Categorization Brief

## Product Notes & Guidelines
- **Naming Convention:** Product names must remain clear, exact, and professional (B2B standard).
- **Compliance:** Do not invent medical claims (focus on terms like "rejuvenation", "contouring", "hydration", "correction").
- **Collection Handles:** To use handles, strengthen the Google indexing for categories.
- **B2B Logic:** "Professional Use Only" should be added as a standard meta-field.
- **Cross-Selling:** Liporase (dissolver) products should be recommended as side product of each Dermal Fillers products "purchased together".
- **Pricing:** Do not hardcode prices in descriptions; rely entirely on dynamic Shopify product data.
