# Content Rules — YaksuDerma

## 1. Purpose

This file defines content, copywriting, compliance, and wording rules for the YaksuDerma Shopify theme.

Codex should use this file when writing or editing:

- Hero copy
- Homepage sections
- Collection descriptions
- Product page text
- Product page accordions
- Trust blocks
- Authenticity sections
- Certificate sections
- Shipping / availability copy
- Customer support copy
- Wholesale inquiry copy
- Product comparison table text
- Related product labels
- Before / after visual section copy
- Button and CTA text
- SEO-supporting category text

The goal is to keep all copy:

- Clear
- Trustworthy
- Professional
- Warm
- Retail-friendly
- Safe from risky medical claims
- Suitable for Korean-origin aesthetics products
- Suitable for international customers

## 2. Core Content Principle

YaksuDerma should communicate:

> Authentic Korean-origin aesthetics products, organized clearly, offered with fair pricing and helpful customer support.

The copy should build trust without exaggeration.

The store should never sound like:

- A medical advice website
- A treatment provider
- A fake luxury brand
- A cheap marketplace
- An aggressive discount store
- A risky claims-heavy product site

## 3. Brand Voice

The YaksuDerma voice should be:

- Warm
- Helpful
- Professional
- Clear
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Human
- Retail-friendly
- International
- Product-discovery focused

The voice should not be:

- Aggressive
- Pushy
- Overly clinical
- Overly luxury
- Overly emotional
- Fear-based
- Claim-heavy
- Marketplace-like

## 5. Words and Phrases to Avoid

Avoid aggressive or risky phrases such as:

```text
Guaranteed results
Instant transformation
Clinically proven
FDA approved
CE certified
KFDA approved
Medical-grade
Permanent results
Fastest shipping
Always in stock
Cheapest on the market
Lowest price guaranteed
Miracle treatment
Fat loss injections
Weight loss shots
Wrinkle removal guaranteed
Use this product for treatment
Required for procedure
Must be used together
```

Do not use these unless the user explicitly provides verified proof and confirms the wording is legally safe.

## 6. Medical Claim Rules

Codex must not invent medical claims.

Do not write copy that claims or implies:

- Treatment outcomes
- Guaranteed beauty results
- Guaranteed medical effects
- Skin regeneration results
- Fat reduction results
- Wrinkle removal results
- Safety guarantees
- Clinical efficacy
- Medical approval
- Product superiority based on clinical claims
- Treatment protocols
- Dosage instructions
- Injection instructions
- Usage instructions for medical procedures

Avoid turning product categories into treatment promises.

## 7. Regulatory and Certification Rules

Do not invent:

- Certificates
- Regulatory approvals
- Import permissions
- FDA approval
- CE approval
- KFDA approval
- Thai FDA approval
- EMA approval
- Country-specific legality
- Supplier authorization
- Clinic-use authorization
- Medical device status
- Prescription status
- Safety certification

If certificate or authenticity content is needed, use neutral wording unless verified details are provided by the user.

Safe wording:

```text
Certificate information may be available where applicable.
```

```text
Contact us for product details and authenticity information.
```

```text
We prioritize original Korean-origin product sourcing and clear product information.
```

Avoid:

```text
All products are FDA approved.
```

```text
Certified medical-grade products.
```

```text
Fully legal in every country.
```

```text
Officially approved for treatment use.
```

## 11. Product Price Rules

Do not hardcode prices into theme copy or Liquid templates.

Prices must come from Shopify product data.

Allowed:

```text
Dynamic Shopify product price
```

```text
Price shown from Shopify product data
```

Not allowed:

```text
Botulax 100 Units is $42 inside hardcoded theme copy
```

Reference prices from catalog documents are for strategy only.

Codex must use:

- `product.price`
- Shopify product data
- Shopify variant pricing
- Shopify dynamic price snippets

## 24. Before / After Content Rules

Before / after visual blocks are sensitive.

Allowed only if:

- The user provides verified images.
- The user confirms compliance.
- The copy is neutral.
- The section is optional and configurable.
- No guaranteed result is implied.

Preferred neutral labels:

```text
Visual reference
Product information image
Before / after reference
Customer-provided visual, if verified
```

Avoid:

```text
Guaranteed transformation
This product creates this result
Instant result
Permanent result
Clinically proven before and after
```

Before / after sections should be disabled by default unless the user provides approved content.

## 31. International Customer Copy

Because YaksuDerma targets Europe first, then Asia and the Middle East, copy should feel internationally understandable.

Use simple, clear English.

Avoid:

- Too much slang
- Country-specific claims
- Overly local references
- Complex regulatory language unless verified
- Unclear abbreviations unless they are product names

## 32. Copy Length Rules

Keep homepage copy short.

Recommended:

- Hero subtext: 1–2 short sentences
- Category card text: 1 short sentence
- Trust block text: 1–2 short sentences
- Product page accordion text: short and expandable
- Collection description: 1 short paragraph

Avoid long dense paragraphs, especially on mobile.

## 34. Localization and Translation Rules

If copy is translated later:

- Preserve product names exactly.
- Do not translate brand/product names unless officially used.
- Keep claims equally safe in every language.
- Avoid adding claims during translation.
- Keep CTAs clear and natural.

## 35. What Codex Should Do Before Writing Copy

Before writing copy, Codex should check:

1. Which page or component the copy is for.
2. Whether the content is homepage, collection, product, support, or wholesale.
3. Whether product-specific claims are being requested.
4. Whether verified data is available.
5. Whether copy should be dynamic or editable through Shopify.
6. Whether the copy could imply a medical claim.
7. Whether it aligns with YaksuDerma’s warm, trustworthy tone.

## 36. What Codex Must Ask Before Using

Codex must ask or flag uncertainty before using:

- Certificate names
- Regulatory approvals
- Medical claims
- Treatment claims
- Before / after claims
- Product safety claims
- Country-specific legal claims
- Manufacturer authorization claims
- Guaranteed shipping claims
- Professional-use restrictions
